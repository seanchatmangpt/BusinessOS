# OSA Build - Onboarding & OS Building Flow
## "Build Your OS" - First Launch Experience

**Date:** January 18, 2026
**Branch:** `feature/ios-desktop-flow-migration`

## 🎯 Core Concept

**When a user installs OSA Build, THIS is how they build their Operating System:**

1. **Install OSA Build** → First launch
2. **Onboarding Flow** → AI analyzes you, generates personalized starter apps
3. **Your OS is Born** → Home screen with YOUR apps that make up YOUR operating system
4. **Build & Expand** → Use conversational AI to create more apps, remix from others
5. **Your Personalized OS** → A living, growing operating system built around YOU

## 🔄 THE COMPLETE USER JOURNEY

### Day 1: Install & Onboard
1. Download OSA Build
2. First launch → Onboarding
3. Sign in with Google
4. Connect Gmail (optional)
5. Choose username
6. OSA analyzes your data
7. OSA generates 4 personalized starter apps
8. **YOU NOW HAVE YOUR FIRST OPERATING SYSTEM** with 4 apps

### Day 1: Explore & Use
9. Open an app (e.g., "Book Finds")
10. Use it to discover books
11. Notice something to improve
12. Right-click → Edit → Chat with OSA
13. "Add a wishlist feature"
14. OSA updates the app
15. **YOUR OS JUST EVOLVED**

### Day 2: Build More
16. Click "+" to build new app
17. "Build me a workout tracker"
18. OSA builds it in seconds
19. **YOUR OS NOW HAS 5 APPS**

### Day 3: Discover & Remix
20. Go to Explore tab
21. See "City Block Builder" by @tykra
22. Click "Remix"
23. Customize it via chat
24. **YOUR OS NOW HAS 6 APPS** (5 original + 1 remixed)

### Week 1: You've Built Your OS
- 12 apps in your OS
- 6 you built from scratch
- 4 you remixed from others
- 2 OSA generated for you
- **This is YOUR personalized Operating System**

## 🎯 KEY REALIZATION

**The "apps" in the iOS flow = The modules/apps that make up the user's Operating System in OSA Build**

When a user "builds their OS," they're:
1. Getting AI-personalized starter apps (onboarding)
2. Building new apps conversationally (chat with OSA)
3. Remixing apps from the community (social discovery)
4. Iterating on their apps over time (evolution)

**The result: A living, growing, personalized Operating System built around the individual user.**
